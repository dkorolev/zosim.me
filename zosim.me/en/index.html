Institutional areas

Although his work is typically for private residences, Vladimir has also designed landscapes for subdivisions, participated in private and public recreational park designs, and completed landscapes for commercial buildings like churches and storefronts.

Vladimir Zosim has over 20 years of experience in all things landscaping. Although initially starting his career in Germany, he established himself in America and has since completed countless landscapes in the the states of Washington, Oregon, and Hawaii.
